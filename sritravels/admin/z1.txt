Matara District
Mirissa

Mirissa is a beautiful coastal town known for its golden beaches, palm trees, and whale watching tours. Visitors can relax by the ocean, enjoy fresh seafood, or take boat trips to see blue whales and dolphins. It’s a perfect getaway for beach lovers.


Kegalle District 
Kitulgala      

Kitulgala is a lush rainforest destination in western Sri Lanka, famous for white-water rafting on the Kelani River. It’s also known for adventure activities like trekking, canyoning, waterfall abseiling, and bird watching. The area gained fame as the filming location for the classic movie The Bridge on the River Kwai. With its tropical scenery and rich biodiversity, Kitulgala is a paradise for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.
